# Social Media Deployment Plan

## Overview

RideCareShare will maintain presence on four major platforms to engage with potential users, share updates, and build community before the mobile app launch.

**Primary Goal**: Build anticipation and community engagement for the RideCareShare mobile app launch.

## Pre-Launch Content Calendar

### Week 1-2: Introduction Phase
- **Facebook**: Page launch, mission statement post, invite friends
- **X**: Introductory thread about the problem RideCareShare solves
- **Instagram**: First post with logo and mission, stories with polls
- **YouTube**: Upload channel trailer

### Week 3-4: Build Awareness
- **Facebook**: Share blog post about loneliness epidemic and transportation costs
- **X**: Daily facts about rideshare industry and charitable giving
- **Instagram**: Visual quotes from founder, infographics about impact
- **YouTube**: Founder story video (3-5 minutes)

### Week 5-6: Engagement Phase
- **Facebook**: Host Q&A session, share user testimonials from waitlist
- **X**: Twitter Spaces discussion about sustainable travel
- **Instagram**: Instagram Live with founder, user story highlights
- **YouTube**: How It Works explainer video

### Week 7-8: Launch Preparation
- **All Platforms**: Countdown to launch, sneak peeks of app
- **Facebook**: Create launch event
- **X**: Live-tweet final preparations
- **Instagram**: Stories countdown, reels showing features
- **YouTube**: App walkthrough video (ready for launch day)

## Social Media Management

### Tools (Recommended)
- **Scheduling**: Buffer (free tier allows 10 scheduled posts)
- **Analytics**: Native platform analytics (free)
- **Design**: Canva (free tier for social media graphics)
- **Link Tracking**: Bitly (optional, for tracking clicks)

### Response Protocol
1. Monitor mentions and comments daily
2. Respond within 24 hours
3. Direct support questions to email (when available)
4. Engage authentically with community
5. Share user-generated content (with permission)

### Metrics to Track
- **Follower Growth**: Weekly tracking
- **Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares per post
- **Click-through Rate**: Website visits from social media
- **Waitlist Signups**: Track source from social media
- **Video Views**: YouTube watch time and completion rate

## Budget Considerations

### Free Tier Strategy (Current)
- All platforms free to create accounts
- Organic growth through quality content
- No paid advertising initially
- Focus on authentic community building

### Future Paid Strategy (Optional)
- **Facebook/Instagram Ads**: $500-1000/month for targeted reach
- **X Promoted Posts**: $200-500/month for visibility
- **YouTube Ads**: $300-700/month for video discovery
- **Total**: ~$1000-2200/month for comprehensive paid strategy

**Recommendation**: Start organic, add paid promotion once you have proven content strategy and initial traction.
